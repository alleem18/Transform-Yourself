TRAINING SPLIT

Following a perfect training split is necesssary for building musclce . The main focus of training split is to train each muscle group atleast twice a week which is a proven science study. Thus at your convinience and avialability of time in a week , the split is desinged . Beginners can really be benifitted if they follow 3 day split for first 1 to 3 months. Every week the training frequency needs to be increased

Now WTF is training frequency ?

CHILL!!! It's just volume x sets x reps . See it's simple

Why to increase though ?
